About Zephyr

Zephyr is annual technical festival of Thakur College of Engineering and Technology. It started back in 2004 with the objective of providing students a platform to enhance and exhibit their technical expertise. Over the course of year sice it's inception, not only has there been a swinging increase in footfall,but event and tournaments. Zephyr is a 3 day grand festival which is attended by attendees from all walks of life including students, professors as well as general public.
